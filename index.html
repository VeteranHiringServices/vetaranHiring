About Us

Bridging Military Talent with Corporate Opportunities

We are a team of veterans and corporate leaders committed to bring Talent from Defence Forces(Military, Navy & Airforce) to civilian world. Our goal is to ensure every veteran seamlessly transitions into meaningful corporate roles. We provide comprehensive support to Veterans (He), Veterans (She), and Veterans with Disabilities (PWD), empowering all to thrive in corporate environments.

Our vision is to establish a world where every veteran experiences significant employment prospects and effortlessly integrates into the corporate workforce.

Veteran Recruiting

 

Why Veterans ?

The Indian Armed Forces develop skilled and capable professionals who emerge as leaders in the corporate world.They bring with them:

  • Proven Leadership and Adaptability
  • Highly Skilled and Trained
  • Teamwork and Collaboration
  • Discipline and Work Ethic
  • Resilience and Adaptability
  • Commitment and Loyalty
  • Ability to Handle Pressure
  • Diversity and Inclusion

    • Which roles typically align best with veteran expertise and experience?

    Tri Services employs them in different areas of work spanning across Technical and Non Technical roles.

  • Technical (mid to senior management) : Telecommunications and Signalling, Electronics, Mechatronics, Electrical, Civil engineering, Engineering
  • Non Technical (mid to senior management) : Sales, Operations,Warehouse/Logistics, Supply Chain, Maintenance Management, Procurement, Transport, Finance, Human Resources
  • Strategic: Executives, Board members, CXOs Their expertise could be employed in various Industries like, Retail, IT, ITES, Manufacturing, Automobile, FMCG, Defence products, Aviation, BFSI, Shipping, Healthcare, Education & Training, Infrastructure, Pharma, etc Volume hiring for special projects for clients across entry to senior levels.

    • Industries:Retail, IT, ITES, Manufacturing, Automobile, FMCG, Defence products, Aviation, BFSI, Shipping, Healthcare, Education & Training, Infrastructure, Pharma, etc Volume hiring for special projects for clients across entry to senior levels

    How their journey looks like at military?

    Selection process:

    Indian Defence forces selection process is one of the intense and toughest process in the world to select the best to contribute to the formidable Tri Services. Starts with UPSC exams where candidates prepare for months/years. 5 days intense interview process (psychometric tests, group tasks to assess personal endurance & leadership abilities, personal interview to get to the core of the person) Once they are inducting into the services they are continuously evaluated on the following parameters

    Once they are inducting into the services they are continuously evaluated on the following parameters during their entire service:

  • Effective Intelligence
  • Reasoning Ability
  • Power Of Expression & Leadership Presence
  • Organising Ability
  • Social Adaptability & Collaboration
  • Self Confidence & Initiative
  • Ability to Influence the Group
  • Agility in Decision Making
  • Integrity & Determination

    • A robust, proven process of selection, training, induction, performance evaluation and leadership development throughout their career gives an edge to prospective employers to induct veterans into their workforce seamlessly.

    How do veterans fit into a DE&I strategy?

    Hiring veterans promotes diversity and inclusion within the workforce. Their varied backgrounds, experiences, and perspectives enrich the corporate culture and contribute to a more dynamic and innovative workplace

    What positions are suitable for veterans within my organisation?

    If you seek to integrate veteran talent into your workforce but are unsure of the initial steps, we invite you to reach out to us today. Our team can provide an assessment to determine the optimal placement of veterans within your organization and engage in discussions regarding the incorporation of veteran recruiting into your strategic initiatives

    How do you establish connections between military roles and civilian positions?

    "Each role within the military seamlessly translates into the civilian workforce. Whether encompassing technical proficiency or leadership skills, veterans are equipped to drive organisational success.” Our military transition recruiting team possesses comprehensive knowledge of the translation of diverse military roles into civilian careers wherein veterans excel.
    Our veteran recruiters specialise in navigating this transition, aiding in the identification and selection of suitable veteran talent tailored to your organization's needs.
