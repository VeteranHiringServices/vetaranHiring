About Us

Bridging Military Talent with Corporate Opportunities

We are a team of veterans and corporate leaders committed to bridging the gap between defence forces(Military, Navy & Airforce) and civilian worlds. Our goal is to ensure every veteran seamlessly transitions into meaningful corporate roles. We provide comprehensive support to Veterans (He), Veterans (She), and Veterans with Disabilities (PWD), empowering all to thrive in corporate environments.

Why Veterans

Proven Leadership and Adaptability, Highly Skilled and Trained, Teamwork and Collaboration

Discipline and Work Ethic, Resilience and Adaptability, Commitment and Loyalty

Ability to Handle Pressure, Diversity and Inclusion

Which roles typically align best with veteran expertise and experience?

Technical (mid to senior management) : Electronics, Mechatronics, Electrical, Telecommunications and Signalling, Civil engineering

Non Technical (mid to senior management) : Sales, Engineering, Operations,Warehouse/Logistics, Supply Chain, Maintenance Management, Procurement, Transport, Finance, Human Resources Strategic: Executives, Board members, CXOs

Industries:Retail, IT, ITES, Manufacturing, Automobile, FMCG, Defence products, Aviation, BFSI, Shipping, Healthcare, Education & Training, Infrastructure, Pharma, etc Volume hiring for special projects for clients across entry to senior levels

How their journey looks like at military?

Selection process:

  • UPSC (one of the toughest entrance exams to clear)
    • 5 days intense interview process (psychometric tests, group tasks to assess personal endurance & leadership abilities, personal interview to get to the core of the person) Continuously evaluated on the following parameters during their entire service:
  • Effective Intelligence
  • Reasoning Ability
  • Power Of Expression
  • Organising Ability
  • Social Adaptability
  • Sense of Responsibility
  • Co-operation
  • Self Confidence
  • Initiative
  • Ability to Influence the Group
  • Speed of making Decision
  • Determination
  • Liveliness
  • Courage
  • Stamina
    • An intense process of induction, performance and evaluation they have experienced would help prospective employers to induct veterans into their workforce seamlessly.

    How do veterans fit into a DE&I strategy?

    Hiring veterans promotes diversity and inclusion within the workforce. Their varied backgrounds, experiences, and perspectives enrich the corporate culture and contribute to a more dynamic and innovative workplace

    What positions are suitable for veterans within my organisation?

    If you seek to integrate veteran talent into your workforce but are unsure of the initial steps, we invite you to reach out to us today. Our team can provide an assessment to determine the optimal placement of veterans within your organization and engage in discussions regarding the incorporation of veteran recruiting into your strategic initiatives

    How do you establish connections between military roles and civilian positions?

    Each role within the military seamlessly translates into the civilian workforce. Whether encompassing technical proficiency such as logistics or encompassing essential soft skills including leadership, discipline, and commitment, veterans are equipped to drive organisational success. Our military transition recruiting team possesses comprehensive knowledge of the translation of diverse military roles into civilian careers wherein veterans excel. Our veteran recruiters specialise in navigating this transition, aiding in the identification and selection of suitable veteran talent tailored to your organization's needs.
